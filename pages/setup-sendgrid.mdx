# Setup SendGrid

Now we have to get our SendGrid key

Create a SendGrid account and grab your **api key** from Settings.

![Mixpanel Project Key](../public/mixpanel-key.png)

Slap it into the .env and you should be good to go. 

Set your email address as a from address in your SendGrid account. You will need to make sure it's verified before it will work.
